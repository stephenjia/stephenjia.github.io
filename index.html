Biography

I obtained my Ph.D degree from PSI/VISICS group of KU Leuven under the supervision of Prof. Tinne Tuytelaars in 2018 and had the opportunity to work with Prof. Efstratios Gavves and Prof. Luc Van Gool. I obtained both my Master and Bachelor degree from Dalian University of Technology. I was supervised by Prof. Huchuan Lu and had the opportunity to work with Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. My current research focuses on integration of computer vision and natural language processing with deep learning, and also image generation.
