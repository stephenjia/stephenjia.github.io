Biography

I am a Ph.D student at VISICS group of KU Leuven starting from 2013 fall. I am working with Tinne Tuytelaars. I obtained both my Master and Bachelor degree from Dalian University of Technology. I was supervised by Prof. Huchuan Lu and had the opportunity to work with Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. My current research focuses on integration of computer vision and natural language processing with deep learning, and also image generation.
